Our Mission

Jehovah Perazim Christian Fellowship, Inc is a community of Holy Spirit filled believers where Praise, worship and Teaching the Word of God is not bound nor compromised. We are commited to invest into the lives of people God has entrusted to our change, helping them to prosper, and become whole spirit, soul and body, which leads to life eternal with our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

The History of Jehovah Perazim Christian Fellowship (JPCF) (Lord of the Breakthrough)


Jehovah Perazim Christian Fellowship (JPCF) was founded by the hand of God through the obedience of Pastor Thomas Bright, Minister Sharon Bright, Pastor Bessie Scott and, Pastor Teresa Gatlin, They all accepted the calling of the Holy Spirit to take a giant leap of faith and begin a Bible-based, nondenominational ministry; thus the founding of JPCF. With only 4 members the ministry started and the faith walk began. .Services were held each Sunday at the home of Senior Pastor Bright and Minister Sharon Bright for approximately eight weeks. In December 2006, Pastor Bright was given a prophetic Word from God that JPCF would have a place of Worship without paying rent. Through a chance meeting Pastor Bright spoke to Ms. Tinney a neighbor who offered her business building for the ministry's use. In January 2007, JPCF was incorporated as a charitable Non-Profit organization and received its 501 (c) (3) status. In January 2008, after a church-wide fast, it was prophesized that JPCF would move into a new building without using any tithes or offerings so God would be glorified. To God be the glory as this prophetic word came to pass. We are extremely grateful to God for the many people who willingly support JPCF in the up-building of God's kingdom on earth through the spreading of the Good News of Jesus Christ. Jehovah Perazim Christian Fellowship is grateful for its present ministries and the many ministries to come in order that God's will be done on the earth as it is in heaven. May the peace that transcends all understanding be yours as we welcome you to join us in worship, fellowship and ministry in the mighty name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

Our vision

Our vision promotes a climate of love; nurturing, strong character, expectations to become responsible empowered productive contributors to the body of Christ and the world in which we live. We will exercise/apply the principles taught and demonstrated by Jesus to disciple and mentor individuals for service. To carry out the "Great Commision," the gospel of Jesus Christ, who is our Lord and King. We submit ourselves to the Headship of Christ, and to the Holy Spirit who enables us to minister in authority and power, as He leads us into all truth. Therefore, the purpose for which we have been called is fulfilled to the glory of God. AMEN! Mark 16: 15-20
